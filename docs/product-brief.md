# AIbeaty Product Brief

## One-Line Definition

AIbeaty is an AI operating system for beauty salons that turns missed demand, weak follow-up, and inconsistent client retention into trackable workflows.

## Who It Is For

- beauty salons
- hair salons
- nail studios
- lash and brow studios
- med-aesthetic and cosmetic studios
- multi-location beauty operators

## Core Buyer Problems

- missed calls and unanswered inbound requests
- leads leaking between Instagram, WhatsApp, forms, and phone
- slow or inconsistent follow-up
- weak rebooking discipline
- poor owner visibility into bookings, no-shows, and recovery
- front-desk overload and operator chaos

## First-Wave Offer

AI transformation for salons, starting from one real workflow:

- missed-call rescue
- lead qualification and routing
- rebooking reminders
- review capture
- owner reporting

## Product Shape

The first sell is not a giant platform replacement. It is a founder-led rollout of one or two workflows that immediately improve revenue capture and salon calm.

## Why This Can Win

- clear ROI language for salon owners
- practical workflow automation instead of vague AI branding
- modular rollout instead of risky full replacement
- ability to combine best-fit AI providers and channels

## Success Signals

- more leads contacted within minutes
- higher booking recovery from missed demand
- stronger repeat booking behavior
- better owner visibility across staff and locations
- less manual coordination load on front desk staff
