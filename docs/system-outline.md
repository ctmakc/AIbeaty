# AIbeaty System Outline

## Product Direction

AIbeaty should become a salon operations layer, not just a landing page with forms.

## Candidate Modules

### 1. Lead Capture

- unify inbound requests from site, phone, Instagram, messengers, and forms
- normalize source and contact data

### 2. Recovery Engine

- trigger follow-up after missed calls or silent inbound messages
- route lead back to booking flow

### 3. Booking Assist

- qualify intent
- suggest services
- hand off to booking system or staff

### 4. Rebooking Engine

- send timed reminders
- detect churn risk
- recover lapsing clients

### 5. Reviews and Retention

- ask for reviews after successful visits
- react to weak satisfaction signals

### 6. Owner Control Layer

- dashboard for bookings recovered
- team response visibility
- location-level performance view

## MVP Suggestion

Start with:

1. lead capture
2. missed-call recovery
3. rebooking reminders
4. owner reporting

That is enough to support a strong landing promise and a realistic first product demo.
