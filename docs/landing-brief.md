# AIbeaty Landing Brief

## Landing Job

Make salon owners feel that this is not another generic agency or chatbot offer. The site should communicate operational AI for beauty businesses in a concrete, commercially believable way.

## Primary Audience

- salon owners
- salon operators
- growth-minded studio founders
- small beauty chains

## Core Message

AIbeaty helps beauty salons capture more demand, respond faster, rebook more consistently, and see what is actually happening inside the salon pipeline.

## Tone

- premium but grounded
- operational, not hypey
- founder-led, practical, credible

## Key Section Ideas

- Hero with clear promise and first CTA
- Operational pain points
- What the first rollout fixes
- Workflow modules
- Before / after salon operations
- Proof or measurable outcome framing
- Founder-led implementation angle
- Demo / audit CTA

## Things To Avoid

- generic "AI for business" copy
- fake dashboards with no relation to salons
- heavy agency language
- vague claims about transformation without workflow examples

## CTA Candidates

- Book AI Audit
- See Salon Workflow Demo
- Fix My Missed Leads
- Reserve Founder Call
